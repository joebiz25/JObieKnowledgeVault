# Stir-Fry Template

A formula, not a recipe. Pick one from each category.

## The Formula

| Component | Options |
| --------- | ------- |
| Protein | Chicken thigh, tofu, shrimp, beef strips |
| Veggies | Bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, carrot, mushrooms |
| Base | Rice, noodles, or rice noodles |
| Sauce | See below |

## All-Purpose Stir-Fry Sauce

Mix together:
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey or brown sugar
- 1 tsp cornstarch (thickens the sauce)
- Optional: grated ginger, sriracha

## Method

1. Cook rice or noodles first, set aside
2. Cook protein in a hot wok with oil, set aside
3. Stir-fry veggies (hardest first: carrots, broccoli → then softer ones)
4. Add protein back in
5. Pour sauce over, toss for 1-2 minutes until glossy
6. Serve over rice/noodles, top with sesame seeds and spring onion

**Total time:** 25 minutes
