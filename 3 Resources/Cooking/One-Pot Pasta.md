# One-Pot Pasta

Dead simple weeknight meal. Everything goes in one pan.

## Ingredients

- 250g pasta (penne or fusilli)
- 250g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 3 cloves garlic, sliced
- Big handful of fresh basil
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Chili flakes to taste
- Salt and pepper
- ~500ml water or stock
- Parmesan to serve

## Method

1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
2. Add garlic, cook 1 minute until fragrant
3. Add cherry tomatoes, cook 3-4 minutes until they start to break down
4. Add pasta directly into the pan with water/stock
5. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally
6. The liquid should reduce into a sauce — add a splash more water if needed
7. Season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes
8. Tear basil over the top, finish with parmesan

**Total time:** 20 minutes
**Serves:** 2
