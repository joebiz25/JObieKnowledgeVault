# Moving — Lessons Learned

## What Worked

- **Packing early** — starting 3 weeks before made moving day almost relaxed
- **Labeling every box** with room + contents meant unpacking was fast
- **The "first night" box** — sheets, towels, coffee, charger, toilet paper, snacks. Lifesaver.
- **Hiring movers** for heavy stuff. Worth every euro. Don't be a hero with a sofa on a staircase.

## What I'd Do Differently

- **Declutter before packing**, not after. I moved boxes I should have thrown away.
- **Set up internet earlier** — 5 days without WiFi was painful. Order it the day you sign the lease.
- **Take measurements of new place** before buying furniture. Had to return a bookshelf that didn't fit.

## Random Tips

- Trash bags are your friend — use them for clothes (still on hangers), soft items, and last-minute stuff
- Keep important documents (lease, ID, insurance) in a bag you carry personally
- Change your address at the municipality within 5 days — it's legally required in the Netherlands
