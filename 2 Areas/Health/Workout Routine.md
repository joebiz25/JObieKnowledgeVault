# Workout Routine

## Schedule

| Day | Focus |
| --- | ----- |
| Monday | Strength — Upper body |
| Tuesday | Walk or bike (30 min+) |
| Wednesday | Strength — Lower body |
| Thursday | Walk or bike (30 min+) |
| Friday | Strength — Full body |
| Weekend | Longer walk, hike, or active rest |

## Upper Body

- Bench press: 4x8
- Barbell rows: 4x8
- Overhead press: 3x10
- Pull-ups: 3x max
- Face pulls: 3x15

## Lower Body

- Squats: 4x8
- Romanian deadlifts: 4x10
- Bulgarian split squats: 3x10 each leg
- Calf raises: 3x15

## Full Body

- Deadlifts: 4x5
- Incline dumbbell press: 3x10
- Weighted lunges: 3x10 each leg
- Dumbbell rows: 3x10
- Plank: 3x 60s

## Notes

- Always warm up with 5 minutes on the rower
- Progressive overload: increase weight when all sets feel comfortable
- Rest 2-3 minutes between heavy compound lifts
