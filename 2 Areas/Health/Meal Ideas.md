# Meal Ideas

## Breakfast

- Overnight oats with banana, peanut butter, and chia seeds
- Scrambled eggs on sourdough with avocado
- Greek yogurt with granola and berries

## Lunch

- Big salad with grilled chicken, feta, and olive oil dressing
- Leftover dinner (always cook extra)
- Whole wheat wrap with hummus, veggies, and turkey

## Dinner

- One-pot pasta (see [[One-Pot Pasta]])
- Stir-fry with rice (see [[Stir-Fry Template]])
- Oven-baked salmon with roasted vegetables
- Chili con carne — big batch, freezes well

## Snacks

- Apple + peanut butter
- Handful of nuts
- Boiled eggs
- Cottage cheese with cucumber

## Principles

- Meal prep on Sundays saves the week
- Focus on protein: aim for 1.6g per kg bodyweight
- Keep it simple — consistency beats perfection
