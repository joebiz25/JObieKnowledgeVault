# Career Goals 2026

## Big Picture

Transition from senior engineer to a role with more leadership and mentoring. Build a public presence through writing and speaking.

## Concrete Goals

- [ ] Give at least 2 presentations (team or meetup level)
- [ ] Contribute to 1 open-source project meaningfully
- [ ] Complete a system design course

## Review Dates

- Mid-year check-in: June
- End-of-year review: December

## Questions to Reflect On

- Am I spending my time on work that compounds, or just staying busy?
- What skill, if I got 20% better at it, would change my career the most?
- Who do I want to learn from this year?
